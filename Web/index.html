Follow the guide below to obtain the thread_ts from a Slack URL:

  1. Open your Slack workspace in a browser.
  2. Navigate to the channel containing the thread you are interested in.
  3. Click on the timestamp of the thread's first message to open it in a new view.
  4. Copy the URL from the address bar. It should look something like: https://hackclub.slack.com/archives/C017YL1NMU0/p1719964952242809?thread_ts=1719963360.956289&cid=C017YL1NMU0
